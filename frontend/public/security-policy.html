Security Policy

At Alfanio LTD, we take the security of our systems and data seriously. This security policy outlines how we handle security vulnerabilities and how security researchers can report issues to us.

Reporting Security Issues

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability in our website, applications, or services, we encourage you to notify us. We welcome working with security researchers to identify and resolve security issues.

Please email your findings to security@alfanio.com. We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 3 business days and strive to send you regular updates about our progress.

Disclosure Policy

  • Please provide detailed reports with reproducible steps. Include the version number of the vulnerable component if applicable.
  • We ask that you give us a reasonable time to investigate and address any findings before public disclosure.
  • Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, and interruption or degradation of our services.
  • Only interact with accounts you own or for which you have explicit permission from the account holder.

Out of Scope

The following test types are excluded from the scope of this policy:

  • Denial of service attacks
  • Spam or social engineering attacks
  • Physical attacks against our offices or data centers
  • Attacks against our employees or users

Safe Harbor

We consider security research conducted under this policy to be:

  • Authorized in accordance with the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
  • Exempt from DMCA restrictions on circumvention of technological measures that control access to copyrighted works
  • Exempt from restrictions in our Terms of Service that would otherwise restrict security research
  • Lawful, helpful to the overall security of the Internet, and conducted in good faith

You are expected, as always, to comply with all applicable laws.

Recognition

We're happy to acknowledge security researchers who have responsibly disclosed valid security issues. With your permission, we'll recognize your contribution on our website.
